01 - About

A foundation built on trust.

I help businesses and individuals navigate assurance and tax with accuracy, care, and a steady hand.

Currently in assurance at Bateman MacKay LLP, I focus on financial reporting, review, and audit engagements - preparing and reviewing working papers, performing substantive testing, completing analytical procedures, and assisting in the preparation of financial statements. I collaborate closely with clients and team members to deliver clear, accurate, and timely results.

Before joining Bateman MacKay, I built extensive experience in corporate, trust, and personal tax compliance, compilation engagements, and complex reconciliations - working heavily with CaseWare, CaseView, Profile, and TaxPrep, preparing multi-entity reports, reconciling investment and foreign currency accounts, and supporting clients through CRA audits and compliance reviews.
